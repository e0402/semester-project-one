Energetica Exhibition on Loan

On loan from the NEMO Science Museum in Amsterdam, the Energetica exhibition is coming to the Community Science Museum. It's a series of installations that allow visitors to experience the power of the elements as we harness them. From solar energy powering lighting, to 'Wind Island' that shows how turbines are able to use and control wind to create power.

This exhibition is free to attend. Please see opening hours for when to visit.

Exhibition dates:

07.02-08.05.2022

Exhibition spaces

Cosmology

Our fantastic exhibition, 'The Sky Above Us', explores the night sky and what we can see and know about the universe around us. Follow the journey of our solar exploration: from early Arab traders, to the latest exploration of the planets in our solar system.

Evolution

Discover the origins of life on this planet and how the species we know today have evolved our time. You can also take some time to meet a few of the creatures who didn't survive, including our Woolly Mammoth, the Dodos, and a number of dinosaurs too.

Biology and Medicine

Our lives are now much improved thanks to modern medicine. Over a series of exhibits we explore the history of medicine and take a look at some major breakthroughs including the discovery of penicillin and the first heart transplant.
