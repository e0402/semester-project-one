Admission

The entrance is free for everyone! There are also guided tours of the museum that leave every hour. These tours are 70 NOK per person and include a handy printed guide of the museum. If you would like to organise a guided tour for your group of 6 or more people, please use the contact form here to contact us, or send us an email/call by clicking here.

Accessibility

The museum has wheelchair accessibility ramps. It also has audio guides and braille display signs for the visually impaired.
